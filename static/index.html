TEA, a Project Workflow Application

Tired of the stresses of starting and completing a group project? Need help finding a group project to join? Or you want to display your project for others to join? Look no further! Check out TEA, a Project Workflow Application. Not only can you join and create projects, TEA will also help you complete your project! Equipped with Milestones, Announcements, a Calendar, and more, your project dashboard will motivate you and make it easy to track your project's progress!
